a tribute to
Ginger Johnson



We adopted Ginger from Petco on October 24, 2005, only one month after we were married. We looked on PetFinder.com to find a dog that would be good for our family. Ginger was almost 2 years old when we got her. We were lucky enough to have her in our lives for 11 years. She lived in three homes with us, welcomed two additional little members to our family and forever left paw prints on our hearts!!

A dog is the only thing on Earth that loves you more than they love themselves!

Josh Billings
